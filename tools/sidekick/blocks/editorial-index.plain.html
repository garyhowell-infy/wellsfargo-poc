01

We go first

Every route published on WKND Adventures has been completed by a WKND contributor before a word is written for public consumption. No desk research. No secondhand accounts.

02

We document everything

A WKND trip report is not a summary. It's a working document: full gear lists, weather windows, permit processes, camp notes, water sources, and honest ground assessments.

03

We update our work

Routes change. Bridges wash out. Access agreements expire. Every report carries a last-verified date — and when conditions shift, we go back and update the record.
